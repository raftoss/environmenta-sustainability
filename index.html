Let's save nature — let's save the future!
  • Climate change
    Climate change means long-term weather changes: warming, ice melting, sea level rise, and more frequent storms. The causes are air pollution and deforestation.
  • Recycling & waste management
    Recycling and waste management involve the processing of waste for re-use or safe disposal. This helps reduce pollution, conserve resources, and decrease waste.
  • Biodiversity
    Biodiversity is the variety of organisms living on Earth, both plants, animals, and microorganisms. It acts as the backbone for an ecosystem to function properly with the good health of this planet. Loss of it threatens nature's stability to support life on Earth.
